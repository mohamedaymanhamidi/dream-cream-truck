Quick Answers

Frequently asked questions about our services

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance for private events. For large weddings or corporate events, 2-3 months is ideal to secure your date.

Do you travel outside the city?

Yes! We serve the greater metro area within a 50-mile radius. Travel fees may apply for distances over 25 miles.

Can we customize the menu?

Absolutely! We love creating custom menus for special events. From vegan options to themed flavors, we can tailor our offerings to your needs.

What are your payment terms?

We require a 50% deposit to secure your booking, with the balance due one week before your event. We accept all major credit cards and checks.
